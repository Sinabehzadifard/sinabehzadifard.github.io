About Me

A dedicated researcher exploring the intersections of sustainability, operations management, and emerging technologies.

My Academic Journey

I'm a PhD Candidate at the Stockholm School of Economics, focusing on Sustainable Operations and Supply Management. My research examines how businesses can optimize their operations while minimizing environmental impact and maximizing social responsibility.

With a background in both academic research and practical industry experience, I bring a unique perspective to understanding and solving complex supply chain challenges in today's rapidly evolving business landscape.

My work has been recognized in various academic journals and conferences, and I'm passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in sustainability and operations management.
